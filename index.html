About bd&company

Founded in 2003, bd&company has established itself as a premier advisory firm specializing in infrastructure development across Sub-Saharan Africa. Our multidisciplinary team brings together economists, financial analysts, engineers, and policy experts with deep regional expertise.

Over the past two decades, we've successfully advised on over 150 infrastructure projects with a combined value exceeding R150 billion. Our work spans 13 African countries, where we've helped governments, private sector entities, and development finance institutions navigate complex infrastructure challenges.

What sets us apart is our unique combination of global best practices with localized solutions tailored to African markets. We understand the regulatory environments, financing constraints, and implementation challenges specific to the region.

Join our Team!

Our advisory services are built on three pillars: rigorous research, practical implementation experience, and innovative financial structuring. This approach has helped our clients secure funding from multilateral institutions, private investors, and development agencies.

We're particularly proud of our track record in:

  • • Structuring bankable PPP projects in challenging environments
  • • Developing climate-resilient infrastructure solutions
  • • Creating innovative financing mechanisms for underserved sectors
  • • Building local capacity through knowledge transfer

Water & Sanitation

18+ Projects R8.2B Managed

Our team has structured water supply and sanitation projects serving over 5 million people. We specialize in developing financially sustainable models for municipal water services, wastewater treatment plants, and irrigation infrastructure that balance affordability with long-term viability.
